Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer catering services?

+

Yes, we offer catering for events of all sizes! Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your specific needs and we'll be happy to accommodate your event.

Do you offer WiFi?

+

Yes, we provide complimentary high-speed WiFi for all our customers. Just ask for the password at the counter when you place your order.

Are your coffee beans available for purchase?

+

Absolutely! We sell our freshly roasted beans by weight. You can purchase them in-store or order online for delivery. We offer a variety of single-origin beans and special blends.

Do you have gluten-free or vegan options?

+

We have a selection of gluten-free pastries and vegan milk alternatives available daily. Our menu clearly marks items that are gluten-free, vegan, or can be modified to suit dietary preferences.
